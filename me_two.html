Qian Zhu

Last summer, my family embarked on a road trip from Boston to San Diego. Initially, we planned to spend a week at Yellowstone National Park, but because of our dog, we chose to drive there instead of flying. Along the way, my daughter expressed interest in visiting the San Diego Zoo, which led us to extend our trip.

My favorite restaurant is the El Tovar Dining Room, nestled in the historic El Tovar Hotel at Grand Canyon National Park. With breathtaking views of the South Rim, the restaurant offers a unique taste of the region, featuring dishes like roasted poblano pepper soup and Colorado rack of lamb.

During our journey, we explored many breathtaking locations such as Yosemite, Antelope Canyon, Death Valley, Zion, and Bryce National Parks. The entire trip took a month to complete, and it was an unforgettable adventure.

My favorite food is a Korean dish called Bibimbap. It is made with pre-steamed rice placed in a stone pot, topped with a variety of vegetables, meats, fried eggs, and Korean hot sauce. To eat Bibimbap, you stir all the ingredients thoroughly and enjoy the mix of flavors!
