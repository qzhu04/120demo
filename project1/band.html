Meet Velvet Harmony

With over 10 years of experience, Velvet Harmony is a professional band that brings a mix of timeless classics and modern hits to every event. Whether it's a wedding, corporate party, or special gathering, we guarantee an unforgettable musical experience.

Emma Westwood

Lead Vocalist

Emma’s voice is the heart and soul of Velvet Harmony. With a background in both jazz and pop, her rich, soulful tone captivates audiences. Emma has performed in numerous musical theater productions and brings her natural stage presence and emotion to every song.

Jason Hart

Guitarist & Backup Vocalist

Jason is Velvet Harmony’s versatile guitarist, skilled in styles ranging from rock to blues and jazz. He plays both acoustic and electric guitar, adding depth to the band’s sound and harmonies that blend with Emma’s powerful vocals.

James Cooper

Jazz Pianist

James Cooper is an acclaimed jazz pianist, blending classic bebop influences with modern jazz harmonies. With a soulful touch and improvisational flair, he has performed at major festivals and venues, captivating audiences worldwide.

Mike Taylor

Jazz Drummer

Mike Taylor is a seasoned jazz drummer with over 15 years of experience, known for his dynamic rhythms and creative improvisation. He has played with top ensembles across the U.S., bringing energy and precision to every performance.
