Meet Velvet Harmony

We’re a band of professional musicians with years of experience, dedicated to making your event unforgettable.

Emma Westwood

Lead Vocalist

Emma’s voice is the heart and soul of Velvet Harmony. With a background in both jazz and pop, her rich, soulful tone captivates audiences. Emma has performed in numerous musical theater productions and brings her natural stage presence and emotion to every song.

Jason Hart

Guitarist & Backup Vocalist

Jason is Velvet Harmony’s versatile guitarist, skilled in styles ranging from rock to blues and jazz. He plays both acoustic and electric guitar, adding depth to the band’s sound and harmonies that blend with Emma’s powerful vocals.

Olivia Chen

Keyboardist & Synth Specialist

Olivia is Velvet Harmony’s secret weapon. With classical piano training and a love for electronic music, Olivia brings a wide range of sounds. She helps arrange many songs and ensures each setlist is tailored for every event.

Mark Johnson

Bassist

Mark’s smooth and steady bass lines hold down the groove, making sure everyone stays on the dance floor. With experience in funk, R&B, and jazz, Mark provides the rhythmic backbone of Velvet Harmony.

Sarah Lopez

Drummer & Percussionist

Sarah is the heartbeat of Velvet Harmony, known for her precise drumming style. With experience in wedding bands and indie rock groups, Sarah’s infectious enthusiasm keeps the energy high at every event.
