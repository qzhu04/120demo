Meet Velvet Harmony

Velvet Harmony brings over a decade of musical expertise to the stage, captivating audiences with their unique blend of genres. Whether performing for weddings, corporate events, or private parties, the band is known for delivering unforgettable live performances. With a passion for music and a commitment to professionalism, Velvet Harmony guarantees to bring energy, emotion, and precision to every event. Our team of seasoned musicians offers the perfect soundtrack to make your special day extraordinary. From jazz to pop and everything in between, Velvet Harmony knows how to create the perfect atmosphere.

Emma Westwood

Lead Vocalist

Emma Westwood is the lead vocalist of Velvet Harmony and the voice that defines the band’s signature sound. With over 12 years of experience in live performance, Emma's captivating voice brings every song to life. A seasoned professional with roots in both jazz and contemporary pop, Emma has performed at over 100 weddings and high-profile events. Her ability to connect with audiences and deliver powerful, emotive performances ensures that every guest is enchanted from the very first note to the last encore.

Jason Hart

Guitarist & Backup Vocalist

Jason Hart is Velvet Harmony’s versatile guitarist and backup vocalist, known for his technical skill and charismatic stage presence. With over 15 years of experience in live bands, Jason has mastered a variety of genres from classic rock to smooth jazz. His ability to seamlessly switch between acoustic and electric guitar allows the band to cover a wide range of musical styles, adding depth and dimension to the band’s live performances. Jason’s harmonies complement Emma’s lead vocals, creating rich, full-bodied soundscapes that captivate audiences.

James Cooper

Jazz Pianist

James Cooper is an internationally recognized jazz pianist, known for his sophisticated playing style that blends modern harmonies with classic jazz melodies. Over the past decade, James has performed at major jazz festivals and luxury events around the globe. His improvisational skills and deep musicality add a layer of elegance to Velvet Harmony’s sound, making each performance unique. Whether it’s a slow ballad or an upbeat number, James’ dynamic piano skills ensure that the music leaves a lasting impression.

Mike Taylor

Jazz Drummer

Mike Taylor is Velvet Harmony’s highly skilled jazz drummer with over 20 years of experience in live performances. His diverse background includes playing with some of the most prestigious jazz ensembles in the country. Mike’s ability to create complex rhythms and adapt to any musical style ensures that the band’s sound remains fresh and exciting. Known for his impeccable timing and improvisational flair, Mike provides the rhythmic foundation that keeps Velvet Harmony’s performances tight and energetic.
